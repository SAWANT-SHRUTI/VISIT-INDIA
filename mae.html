Mae De Deus Church

About Mae De Deus Church in Goa One of the prettiest churches, Church of Mae De Deus is situated in Saligao, Bardez. The popular church is set amidst picture perfect settings of Goaâ€™s countryside and boasts Neo-Gothic architecture style. This popular church in Goa is known for housing the miraculous statue of Mae de Deus (Mother of God) brought from the ruins of the convent of Mae de Deus at Old Goa. Amongst other attractions of this church in Goa is its gothic style spires. In the evening, all light up, Church of Mae De Deus looks quite a sight to see in Goa. On the first Sunday of the month of May, the church also celebrates the feast of Our Lady, Mother God. On this occasion, a grand procession followed by a firework display take place.

Visiting Time of Church of Mae De Deus Monday to Sunday: 9.00am to 12.30pm and from 3.00pm to 5.00pm.

How to Reach Church of Mae De Deus? The Church of Mae De Deus is situated around 13.3 km from Panaji via Chogm Rd and NH 66. One can easily get a taxi or an auto-rickshaw to reach the Church of Mae De Deus from Panaji.
